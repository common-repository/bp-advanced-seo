BP Advanced SEO does all the SEO for your BuddyPress site, including blog posts.

== Description ==

BP Advanced SEO handles the SEO not only for the BuddyPress part of your site, but also for your blog posts.
Descriptions are being generated automatically, if you choose not to write your own. Keywords will be generated
by combining post tags and user-input keywords.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the files to wp-content/plugins/bp-advanced-seo

2. Activate the plugin in your admin backend

3. Go to BuddyPress->BP Advanced SEO and change the options to your liking

That's it ... enjoy!
